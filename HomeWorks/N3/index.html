Takeaways from the state of remote work in 2020

One of the best and most recent comprehensive studies on remote work is Buffer and AngelList's report titled The State of Remote Work in 2020. The report is full of rich remote work statistics and important information, so I encourage you to read it yourself if you're looking for more data.
You can read the full article (in Russian) at the link.
98% of people would like to work remotely at least some of the time.
97% of people would recommend remote work to others.
