About the company

The company was founded by Dirk Schmidt and Michael Brause in Leipzig. Thanks to our vast network to doctors and hospitals we are able to offer the best match for our clients.

Health of our clients is always the most important. You can be confident that your health is in good hands. To achieve this target, we do our best so you can finish the program fast and smooth.

We are the interface for our customers from all over the world to the renowned medical location Germany.
